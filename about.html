ABOUT

I am a multidisciplinary designer and visual artist with over 18 years of experience collaborating with international brands. My practice spans graphic design, interactive media, motion design, and branding, alongside a strong passion for Color & Material Design in footwear, where I explore colorways, material concepts, and product visuals.

My work blends creative experimentation with precise execution and has been featured in international competitions as well as collaborations with global brands.

Based in Berlin, I am open to commissions, exhibitions, and creative partnerships.
