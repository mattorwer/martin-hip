Echo Flow

Vimeo video blocked due to cookie preferences.
Accept cookies to watch videos

Echo Flow is an experimental video tool that creates a visual echo of any video input, revealing a short lapse of past frames layered in sequence. The effect is rendered in real time from either linked video files or live webcam input. A control panel lets users adjust the delay and shape the visual output. Audio input can also be linked to transform and position the video based on measured frequencies, creating dynamic, sound-responsive distortions and movement.
