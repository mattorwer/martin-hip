NY 90

The NY 90 is a modern take on the classic court silhouette. For the women’s release in Summer 2022, we elevated the design with subtle pastel accents and a sleek Trefoil metal detail, giving the timeless style a more exclusive and elegant feel.

For the men’s release, the NY 90 features a perforated toe cap with suede overlays and a sporty nylon tongue. A cut-out window logo and off-white colorways with sport-inspired pops give the classic silhouette a fresh update.

Campus 00s

I worked on the first women’s release of the Campus 00s for Summer 2023. The adicolor pack features grey suede with contrast stripes, tonal details, and a gum outsole.

This pack combines a rich suede upper with contrasting pastel colors, creating a fresh yet timeless look. A metallic Trefoil medallion on the tongue and zig-zag textured wide laces add bold and sophisticated details.
