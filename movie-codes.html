Movie Codes

Movie Codes is an interactive tool that arranges frames of a movie in sequence and analyzes their colors to create simplified color profiles. These profiles can be used to sort, compare, and visualize film sequences in a structured way. As a small side project, I created large-format posters that assemble every frame of a film into a detailed mosaic, revealing its colors, pacing, and overall visual structure.
