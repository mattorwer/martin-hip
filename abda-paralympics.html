TV-Trailer – Paralympics

Vimeo video blocked due to cookie preferences.
Accept cookies to watch videos

With the agency Zum Goldenen Hirschen, I developed a TV spot concept using my Recreating Movement motion visualizations. Filmed in a large Berlin factory hall, I directed the setup, planning, and shoot. The trailer for the 2008 Beijing Paralympics features German athlete Heinrich Popow in long jump and sprint, with visualizations highlighting the dynamic complexity of his movements.

TV Trailer, Motion Visualization

Role:
