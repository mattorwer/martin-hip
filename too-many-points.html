Too Many Points

Vimeo video blocked due to cookie preferences.
Accept cookies to watch videos

Too Many Points is an experimental video tool that transforms pixel data from film sequences or live webcam input into immersive three-dimensional worlds in real time. It measures the color values of every pixel, then rearranges them in a virtual 3D space, where users can manipulate the image with filters, parameters, and shape modes – creating dynamic, abstract visuals to explore and zoom into. This makes it a unique fusion of video processing, generative design, and interactive 3D visualization – a playground for artists, designers, and anyone fascinated by the hidden patterns of moving images. The tool supports vector export, enabling generative visuals to be transformed into scalable, high-resolution artworks

Project Type:

Tools:
