Moframes

Vimeo video blocked due to cookie preferences.
Accept cookies to watch videos

Moframes – making movement complexity visible – is a compact online archive dedicated to the study of sports motion. Its visualizations build on techniques from my Recreating Movement project – a tool that extracts individual frames from a film sequence and arranges them sequentially in three-dimensional space. This spatial representation of time reveals intricate patterns and offers unique insights into the complexity of human movement. moframes.net is currently offline.
